# Resources: Vectors, subscripts, and the big Σ

All links verified 2026-07-02.

- 📺 [Khan Academy: Summation notation](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/ap-calculus-ab/ab-integration-new/ab-6-3/v/sigma-notation-sum)
  (video) — Sal builds Σ notation from a plain "add these up" list, at whiteboard
  pace. Watch after the lesson's toy example if the Σ still feels foreign.
- 📖 [Khan Academy: Summation notation review](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/ap-calculus-ab/ab-integration-new/ab-6-3/a/review-summation-notation)
  (short article + practice) — a one-page cheat sheet of the notation with worked
  mini-examples; good to keep open while doing the practice problems.
- 📺 [3Blue1Brown: Vectors, what even are they?](https://www.3blue1brown.com/lessons/vectors)
  (video + article, Essence of Linear Algebra ch. 1) — beautiful animated intuition
  for vectors as arrows AND as lists of numbers. The "list of numbers" view is
  exactly the R-vector view this module uses; the arrow view will pay off when a
  future course needs real linear algebra.
