# Resources: Variance structure & standardization

All links verified 2026-07-04.

- 📺 [Khan Academy — Z-score introduction](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5S-Zfa-vOXs)
  (video, ~7 min) — the "how many standard deviations from the mean" idea, worked on
  small numbers exactly like this lesson's z-score section. Watch after "A tiny example
  first: standardizing" to hear the same recipe from another voice.
- 📺 [StatQuest — Covariance, Clearly Explained!!!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qtaqvPAeEJY)
  (video, ~22 min) — Josh Starmer builds covariance from tiny numeric examples and
  stresses that its *size* is not interpretable on its own — the exact point behind our
  covariance matrix and Trap 1 (why you switch to correlation to compare). Watch after
  "A second example: the covariance matrix."
- 📺 [Khan Academy — ANOVA 2: Calculating SSW and SSB (within and between sums of squares)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=j9ZPMlVHJVs)
  (video, ~13 min) — the within-group / between-group split worked by hand on a small
  dataset, showing they add back to the total — exactly this lesson's "splitting the
  spread" section, numbers-first. Watch after that section; ignore the F-statistic at the
  very end (that's course-level material beyond this foundation).
