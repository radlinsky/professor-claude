# Resources: Bagging, random forests & boosting

All links verified 2026-07-12.

- 📺 [StatQuest: Random Forests Part 1 — Building, Using and Evaluating](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J4Wdy0Wc_xQ)
  (video) --- Josh Starmer builds a random forest from scratch: bootstrapping the
  data, restricting predictors at each split, and evaluating with OOB error. Watch
  after the lesson to see the same bagging-to-RF progression animated.
- 📖 [ISLR §8.2 — Bagging, Random Forests, Boosting, BART](https://hastie.su.domains/ISLR2/ISLRv2_corrected_June_2023.pdf)
  (book, PDF) --- the textbook treatment this module follows: bagging and OOB error
  (§8.2.1), random forests (§8.2.2), boosting (§8.2.3), and BART (§8.2.4). Read
  after the lesson for the formal algorithms and the Heart/gene-expression examples.
- 📺 [StatQuest: Gradient Boost Part 1 — Regression Main Ideas](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3CC4N4z3GJc)
  (video) --- focuses on gradient boosting for regression: fitting trees to
  residuals with a learning rate. Watch after the boosting section to see the same
  sequential residual-fitting idea with a different worked example.
