# Resources: Partial derivatives, and the gradient

All links verified 2026-07-04.

- 📺 [Khan Academy — Partial derivatives, introduction](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AXqhWeUEtQU)
  (video, ~10 min) — walks the core idea of this lesson: how a function's slope depends on
  *which* input you wiggle, so you freeze the others and read one direction at a time. Watch
  after the "A tiny example first" section to see "hold the others still" drawn out.
- 📺 [Khan Academy — Gradient 1 (Partial derivatives, gradient, divergence, curl)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U7HQ_G_N6vo)
  (video, ~10 min) — collects the partials into the gradient vector and shows it pointing
  uphill, exactly this lesson's $\nabla f$. Watch after "The formal version"; you can ignore
  the divergence/curl material later in the playlist — the gradient half is what you need.
